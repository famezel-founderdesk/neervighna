The Tridosha System

Vata, Pitta & Kapha

Ayurveda is based on the balance of three fundamental energies that govern your body and mind.

Air & Space

Vata

Governs movement, breathing, and nerve impulses. When balanced, it brings creativity and flexibility.

Fire & Water

Pitta

Governs digestion, metabolism, and body temperature. When balanced, it brings intelligence and courage.

Earth & Water

Kapha

Governs structure, lubrication, and stability. When balanced, it brings love, calmness, and forgiveness.
