Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first Ayurvedic consultation?
Your first consultation involves a comprehensive assessment of your physical, mental, and emotional health. Our Ayurvedic practitioners will evaluate your current lifestyle, diet, and any specific ailments to determine your unique body constitution (Prakriti) and the balance of your Doshas.
Are Ayurvedic treatments safe?
Yes, when prescribed and guided by experienced practitioners, Ayurvedic treatments are highly safe. We use pure, chemical-free herbal medicines and traditional therapies that work harmoniously with your body to promote natural healing without harsh side effects.
How long does it take to see results?
Ayurveda focuses on addressing the root cause rather than just masking symptoms, which means results can vary. Some patients experience relief within a few days, while chronic conditions might take several weeks or months of consistent adherence to the prescribed diet and therapies.
Do I need to change my entire diet?
We believe in gradual, sustainable changes. While diet plays a crucial role in Ayurveda, our experts will provide personalized, practical dietary advice that aligns with your lifestyle. The goal is balance, not restriction!
