Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the doctor behind Neervighna Ayurveda?

Neervighna Ayurveda is led by a qualified Ayurvedic doctor with deep expertise in classical Ayurveda and modern clinical practice.

All consultations and treatments are personally supervised to ensure safety, authenticity, and effectiveness, blending ancient wisdom with modern care protocols.

Do you provide personalized treatment plans?

Yes. Every treatment plan is customized based on individual Prakriti (constitution), health condition, digestion, lifestyle, and medical history.

Ayurveda does not follow a one-size-fits-all approach; we tailor every herb and therapy to your specific needs.

Is online consultation available?

Yes, Neervighna Ayurveda offers comprehensive online consultations via secure video calls for patients who cannot visit in person.

We perform detailed case evaluation, tongue diagnosis (via photo), and provide digital prescriptions and follow-up care.

Are medical reports required?

Medical reports are helpful but not mandatory. Our primary diagnosis focuses on Ayurvedic clinical examination, pulse diagnosis (Nadi Pariksha), and detailed case history.

However, if you have recent blood work or imaging, please share it to help us understand the full clinical picture.

How do you ensure safety of treatments?

All medicines and therapies are prescribed strictly according to classical Ayurvedic texts and under doctor supervision.

We prioritize quality, correct dosage, and patient safety at every step. We only use certified, heavy-metal tested herbs and formulations.

How often are follow-up consultations needed?

Follow-ups are generally scheduled every 2–4 weeks depending on the severity of the condition and the phase of treatment.

Chronic conditions may require longer monitoring to ensure root-cause healing and prevent recurrence.
