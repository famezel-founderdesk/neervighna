Meet the Founder

Dr. Nihar Zankat

Dr. Zankat believes that true health is not merely the absence of disease, but a state of vibrant physical, mental, and spiritual wellbeing. With over a decade of clinical experience, he has dedicated his life to making the profound wisdom of Ayurveda accessible to the modern world.

Through Neervighna, he provides personalized care that goes beyond symptom management, addressing the root cause of imbalance. "Neervighna" translates to the remover of obstacles—just as water finds its way around any impediment, his treatments restore the natural flow of energy within your body.
